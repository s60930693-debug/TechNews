Android Security Alert: Over 40% Phones Are Now at Risk

By TechPulse Editorial Team · Published February 2026

Google has issued a serious warning to Android users worldwide: more than 40% of Android devices are no longer protected against modern malware and spyware threats.

Why Are So Many Devices Vulnerable?

Android phones running versions older than Android 13 no longer receive full security updates. This leaves over a billion devices exposed to newly discovered vulnerabilities and active spyware campaigns.

Real-World Consequences

Security researchers confirm that attackers actively exploit outdated Android versions. These attacks can result in data theft, financial fraud, and unauthorized surveillance.

What Should Users Do?

Google strongly advises users to upgrade to phones that still receive security patches. Mid-range phones with guaranteed updates often provide better long-term security than older flagship models.

The Hidden Cost of Fragile Automation

Traditional browser automation frameworks depend on brittle scripts tied directly to UI elements. Minor UI updates break workflows. Agent-based systems introduce latency, cost, and nondeterminism.

Why Data Generation Needs a Rethink
